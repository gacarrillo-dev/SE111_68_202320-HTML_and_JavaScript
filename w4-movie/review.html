Gladiator

ABOUT CAST REVIEW

Review of Empire Records

Gabriel Carrillo

February 7th 2023

I thoroughly enjoyed the movie Gladiator. What I liked most about it was the powerful performance by Russell Crowe as Maximus and the visually stunning battle scenes. The storyline was engaging and kept me on the edge of my seat, and the cinematography was beautiful. I was also impressed by the strong performances from the entire cast, particularly Joaquin Phoenix as the corrupt emperor Commodus.

Overall, I would highly recommend this film to fans of historical dramas and action films. The combination of thrilling battles, compelling characters, and an emotionally charged story make Gladiator a timeless classic.

Gladiator Trivia

  • Joaquin Phoenix ad-libbed screm of "Am I not merciful?" Connie Nielsen wasn't expecting it, and her frightened reaction was genuine.
  • Maximus' description of his home--specifically, how the kitchen is arranged and smells in the morning and at night--was ad-libbed. It's a description of Russell Crowe's own home in Australia.
  • Some of the characters in “Gladiator” are real people from history, such as Commodus and Marcus Aurelius. Maximus, the main character, is not one of those.
